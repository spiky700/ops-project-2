OPS Plan Overview - Jil Shah

Purpose of Plan

Internal operations guide for help desk improvements

Mission Statement

Empowering support for seamless user experience.

Vision Statement

Leading proactive, user-centered support solutions.

SMART Goals for the Year

Goal 1: Reduce response time by 15% – Faster issue resolution.
Goal 2: Achieve 90% customer satisfaction rate – Greater support satisfaction.
Goal 3: Expand self-service options by 20% – Increased self-help access.
Goal 4: Conduct monthly feedback sessions with users – Incorporates user voice.
Goal 5: Enhance team skills through quarterly training – Improved support quality.

Open to Feedback
